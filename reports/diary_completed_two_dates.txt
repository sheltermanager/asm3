Shows all completed diary notes between two dates.
###
en
###
SELECT *
FROM diary
WHERE DateCompleted Is Not Null
AND DateCompleted >= '$ASK DATE Notes completed between$' AND DateCompleted <= '$ASK DATE and$'
ORDER BY DiaryForName, DateCompleted
